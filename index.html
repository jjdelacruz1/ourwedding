About Us

Jerome Dela Cruz

Jerome was born in Canada and moved to the United States at the age of 12. He is outgoing and loves to make people laugh as much as possible. He loves to run and bike. He volunteereed at the Houston SPCA and ended up adopting 3 wonderful dogs named Rex, Rascal and Buddy.

Auggie Ocampo

Auggie was born and raised in the Philippines. She moved to the United States 18 years ago. She is very outgoing, friendly, loves to cook and eat food. Six years ago, she found her passion in Dragon Boating. Aside from work, most of her time is spent serving the community.

Our Story

When you find the right one, you just know it. When you love the right one, you feel it.

  • First We Meet

    September 11, 2020

    Jerome & Auggie first met on September 11, 2020. Their plan was to have a quick meet up at a dessert place, but ended up having dinner at Pappasito's and continued with a nightlong chat at Levy Park.

  • First Vacation

    October 24, 2020

    After officially dating for a month and a half, they both decided to spend a weekend outside of their comfort zones. They stayed at the Marriott Resort in San Antonio, TX. During this mini-vacation they both talked about their future plans together.

  • First Christmas

    December 25, 2020

    Our families first met at the Dela Cruz' family Christmas Dinner in Kingwood. Jokes and laughters were shared over a sumptous meal prepared by the Dela Cruz Family.

  • The Proposal

    January 2, 2021

    Jerome decided to have a photo shoot with Auggie at the Eastern Glades in Memorial Park. Little did Auggie know that this was just a ploy. January 2, 2021 at 5:30pm, Jerome bent his knee and popped the big question - Will you Marry me?
